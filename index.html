📋 MemoCare

The Smart Acknowledgment System for Seamless Communication

✨ Why MemoCare?

🏢 For Organizations

Ensure seamless communication and track engagement effortlessly.

👨‍💼 For Admins

Monitor memos, track acknowledgments, and recognize contributors.

👥 For Users

Stay informed, acknowledge important updates, and get recognized.

📖 How MemoCare Works

🔑 Step 1: Secure Login

Users authenticate with Firebase login to access memos securely.

📝 Step 2: Memo Creation

Admins create and assign memos to ensure structured communication.

✅ Step 3: Acknowledgment Tracking

Users confirm memo receipt, making communication transparent.

📊 Step 4: Engagement Insights

Admins analyze acknowledgment trends and reward responsiveness.
